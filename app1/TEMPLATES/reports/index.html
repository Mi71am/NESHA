One Contribution. Many Hands.

Every contribution begins with generosity and ends with opportunity. Here's how each meal finds its way to the learner who needs it most.

🤝 SHARE

A meal purchased today can become hope for tomorrow. Through NESHA, campus communities can extend every purchase beyond themselves by contributing to a shared meal support fund.

🏫 VERIFY

Partner schools identify learners facing genuine food insecurity. Every recommendation goes through a structured eligibility assessment so support reaches those who need it most.

🍽️ NOURISH

Approved contributions are transformed into meal support using the current meal value of Ksh {{ average_meal_cost|floatformat:0 }}. Support is then allocated digitally through participating schools, ready for Tap2Eat redemption.

🌱 RISE

Every complete meal reaches a learner. Any remaining balance is preserved and carried into the next distribution cycle so every contribution keeps moving toward its purpose.
